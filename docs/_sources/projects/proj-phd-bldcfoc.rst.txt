FoC controller for BLDC motor
==============================
Field Oriented Controller for brushless dc motor (for Parkour Car Project)

.. image:: ../media/uconstruction.jpeg
    :width: 400
    :align: center

The final PCB was very stable, even if motor was changing direction at high speed (step change) it would still be handle all noise from the motor.

Actually the tracks on the PCB would heat up during step direction change which you can see in the following video
