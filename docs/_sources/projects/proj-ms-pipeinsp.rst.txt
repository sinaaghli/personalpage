An IP67 Sewer/Water Pipe inspection robot 
==========================================
I had the honor to start a company with two other teammate at the same time I was in graduate school where we designed a sewer inspection robot wihch could be fully submerged in water. Eventhough we build an amazing robot but business side of things didn't go as expected.

Robot Features

* IP67 robot body
* Differential drive system with brushless motors
* Velocity and Torqu controllers on bldc
* 10x zoom camera head
